A Wedding Invitation

A romantic and timeless wedding invitation designed for John Carl and Laarnie’s special day. This set includes custom visuals, elegant typography, and a cohesive theme that reflects their love story and celebration.

Year

2025

Client

Ms. Dolot

Services

Invitation Layout

Project

Creative

Description

This wedding invitation suite blends modern aesthetics with classical elegance. From the floral borders to the serif typography, each detail was carefully designed to evoke sophistication and romance.

The invitations were printed on soft matte stock, with rich neutral tones and subtle texture that complements the couple's serene theme. The inclusion of custom photography, carefully edited to match the palette, adds a personal and heartfelt touch.

This design set includes a “Save the Date” layout and a formal wedding invitation. Both designs reflect the couple’s story while maintaining visual harmony through color, layout, and type hierarchy.
